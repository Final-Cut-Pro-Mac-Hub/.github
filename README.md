## About

**Final Cut Pro Toolkit** gives you instant access to studio-grade editing assets. Use **MotionVFX plugins**, **custom titles**, **color grades**, **transitions**, and **project templates** — all optimized for Apple Silicon.

- `templates/` — Cinematic, Vlog, Wedding, Corporate
- `plugins/` — mLUTs, mTransition, mTitle packs
- `luts/` — 100+ Film Emulation & Creative LUTs
- `titles/` — Animated lower thirds & intros
- `sound/` — Royalty-free SFX & music stems
- `guides/` — 30-second setup, Apple Silicon optimization
- `assets/` — 8K proxies, keyboard shortcuts
